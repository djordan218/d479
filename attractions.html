Attractions

Come see what all the talk is about!

Merriton Landing Pub Crawl

Explore the lively nightlife of Taniti at Merriton Landing. This guided pub crawl takes you through several popular spots, including a local microbrewery, traditional pubs, and the island's newest dance club. Enjoy local brews, meet fellow travelers, and experience the vibrant atmosphere of Taniti’s night scene.

Yellow Leaf Bay Snorkeling Adventure

Discover the underwater beauty of Taniti at Yellow Leaf Bay. This snorkeling tour offers a chance to see vibrant coral reefs, colorful fish, and other marine life. Perfect for both beginners and experienced snorkelers, this adventure lets you explore the crystal-clear waters that surround the island.

Taniti City Architecture & Beach Tour

Take a guided tour through the heart of Taniti City, where you’ll explore stunning native architecture and learn about the island's rich history. The tour ends with a relaxing stroll along the white sandy beaches of Yellow Leaf Bay, where you can soak up the sun and enjoy the serene ocean views.

Volcano Hike & Rainforest Zip-Lining

Experience the thrill of adventure with this combo tour. Start with a hike up Taniti’s active volcano, where you’ll learn about the island's geological history. Then, zip-line through the lush rainforest canopy, taking in breathtaking views and spotting exotic wildlife along the way.

Chartered Fishing & Helicopter Tour

Combine two of Taniti’s most popular activities in one unforgettable day. Start with a morning chartered fishing trip, where you can catch local fish and enjoy the peaceful ocean. In the afternoon, take to the skies in a helicopter ride that offers a bird’s-eye view of the island’s stunning landscapes, including beaches, forests, and the volcano.

Taniti History Museum & Art Gallery Visit

Immerse yourself in Taniti’s culture with a visit to the local history museum, where you can learn about the island’s past, including its native traditions and colonial history. Afterward, explore the island’s art scene by visiting several galleries showcasing works by local artists, ranging from traditional crafts to modern paintings.
